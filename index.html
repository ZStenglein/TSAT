The Numbers

The Problem

There is a shortage of resources and computer science teachers in Texas.

Significance

There are open jobs in computer science and it is the industry that is growing most rapidly in the United States today. This industry can provide upward mobility for everyone.

Solutions

Invest funding in computer science infrastructure or teacher training programs, and support current bills to do so.
